## Recommended Sequence

1. Resolve the committed PDF mismatch.
2. Complete exact reference formatting and safety review.
3. Decide personal-manual versus general-publication positioning.
4. Resolve placeholders and naming inconsistencies.
5. Perform copy edit and final layout pass.
6. Produce a final candidate PDF for one last proof before any status changes.
